Intro to Traditional Irish Music
.ical
02-24, 10:00–11:00 (America/Chicago), Mitchell

“Intro to Traditional Irish Music” is a hybrid presentation AND performance panel that takes the inner workings of fine details of traditional Irish music and explains them with accessible language accompanied by live performance of various Irish tunes. The presentation aims to cover and explain the types of Irish tunes (such as fast reels, or slow airs) and how each is different; and also bring a visual explanation of how ornamentation is played on the Irish flute specifically. Attendees get to enjoy both a presentation and a variety of music all in one.
